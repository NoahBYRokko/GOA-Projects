Natural Wonders of Japan:

  • Mount Fuji.

  • Mount Fuji is Japan's highest mountain
    and an iconic symbol of the country.
    Here are some interesting facts about
    this majestic mountain. Mount Fuji is
    an active volcano at 3,776 meters
    above sea level.


  • Nikko National Park.

  • Nikko National Park is a UNESCO
    World Heritage Site located in
    Tochigi Prefecture, Japan.
    The park is home to numerous
    temples, shrines, waterfalls,
    lakes, and hiking trails.


  • Yoshino Cherry Blossom.

  • The Yoshino Cherry Blossom is a
    popular and iconic species of cherry
    blossom in Japan, known for its
    delicate pink petals and sweet fragrance.
    Yoshino cherry blossoms are known
    for their sweet, slightly tart flavor
    and are often eaten as a snack
    or used in desserts
