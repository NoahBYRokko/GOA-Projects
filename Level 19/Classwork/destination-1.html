Paris, the City of Light


Natural Wonders of Paris:

  • Eiffel Tower.

  • The Eiffel Tower is an iron lattice
    tower located in Paris, France,
    built for the 1889 World's Fair.
    The Eiffel Tower was designed
    and built by Gustave Eiffel and
    his company, Compagnie des
    Établissements Eiffel


  • Louvre Museum.

  • The Louvre Museumis one of
    the world's largest and most
    famous museums, located in
    the heart of Paris, France.
    The Louvre was originally a
    fortress built in the 12th century.
    In the 14th century, it became a
    royal palace, and in the 16th
    century, it was converted into a museum.


  • Notre-Dame Cathedral.

  • The Notre-Dame Cathedral
    is one of the most iconic and
    historic churches in the world,
    located on the Île de la Cité
    in the heart of Paris, France.
    Construction began in 1163
    and took nearly 200 years
    to complete. It has been
    the site of numerous
    coronations, royal weddings,
    and state funerals.
